Dahou

UI/UX Design
Client － MBI Lab, NCKU
2017.10

"Dahou" in Chinese means to join a meal as a partner with someone. This is an app designed for people to join others meal easier.


Dahou

"Dahou" has three tabs. One for everybody to join meals, one for chefs to release the date, place and meals to eat and the other for users to check the app settings.


Tab: Join Meals

For users to join meals, check vacancies, place and the price. Users can also use filter to screen out the meal type they want.


Tab: Release Meals

For chefs to release information, including meals, vacancies, place and the price. Using attracting photos to catch everybody's eyes.


Tab: Settings

Settings tab allows users to turn on/off the notification, giving them the ability to check the meals joined and joining.
